Module 2: Developing a Timeline and Project Scope Statement

Module 2: Overview

Module Learning Objectives

Upon successful completion of this module, you will be able to:

  • Develop comprehensive project scope statements that define objectives, deliverables, constraints, and boundaries
  • Create realistic project timelines using work breakdown structures and time estimation techniques
  • Integrate scope and timeline requirements to create feasible project plans and manage trade-offs
  • Communicate project parameters effectively to stakeholders and implement change management processes

Module Summary

Effective project management in instructional design begins with two fundamental documents: a comprehensive project scope statement and a realistic timeline. These foundational tools serve as the roadmap for your entire project, establishing clear boundaries, expectations, and schedules that guide decision-making throughout the development process. Research consistently shows that projects with well-defined scope statements and realistic timelines are 70% more likely to be completed successfully within budget and on schedule (Project Management Institute, 2021).

For instructional designers working on online course development, these documents become even more critical due to the complex, iterative nature of educational content creation and the multiple stakeholders typically involved in such projects (Greer & Lin, 2021). This module will guide you through the process of creating both documents, providing practical strategies and templates that can be adapted to various instructional design contexts.
